BUILD A COMPLETE MOBILE APPLICATION: “BreedLog (by Kwantam)”

CORE PURPOSE (VERY IMPORTANT – PRIORITY ORDER)

BreedLog is a PRIVATE, OFFLINE-FIRST livestock breeding and management app for sheep farmers.
The PRIMARY purpose of the app is:
1) To RECORD, MANAGE, and TRACE BREEDING LINEAGES (family trees, bloodlines, sire/dam connections).
2) To STORE COMPLETE ANIMAL DATA (performance, reproduction, health, traits).
3) To ASSIST WITH EVALUATION (manual classing + optional AI valuation as a secondary tool).

This is NOT primarily an AI app.
It is a BREEDING RECORD SYSTEM with genealogy at its core.

The app must function fully OFFLINE for all core features.
Only AI valuation requires internet and login.

- dam_id (FK → animals.id)
- sire_id (FK → animals.id)
- breeder_name

TABLE: breeding_events

TABLE: performance_records
